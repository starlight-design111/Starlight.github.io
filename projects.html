VRChat: Cyber-Lounge Interior Study

Role: Technical Artist & Environment Designer

Tools: Unity 2022, VRChat SDK, Baked Lighting, Light Probes

Technical Highlights:

  • Light Probe Integration: Placed custom light probe networks to ensure dynamic avatars receive accurate color data from emissive wall panels.
  • Baked Lighting Optimization: Utilized Unity's Progressive Lightmapper to achieve high-fidelity shadows while maintaining 90+ FPS for VR users.
  • Atmospheric Composition: Balanced high-contrast emissive textures with reflective surfaces to create a futuristic social space.
